# Track 1 — Form the Virginia Nonprofit Corporation

**Entity (working name):** Save Snakes, Inc.
**Domain:** savesnakes.org
**Location:** Richmond, VA
**Purpose:** Free, no-kill snake rescue & relocation + community education across Greater Richmond.

> ⚠️ Not legal advice. This is the standard Virginia process. Verify all fees on official sites before paying, and have a VA attorney review the Articles + Bylaws before filing if you can.

---

## Step-by-step

- [ ] **1. Confirm the legal name**
  - Search the SCC database: https://cis.scc.virginia.gov (Business Entity Search)
  - Must be distinguishable from existing VA entities. Avoid words implying a different entity type (bank, etc.).
  - Decide exact form: `Save Snakes, Inc.` (recommended) vs `Save Snakes Foundation` etc.
  - Confirm domain/social handles still align.

- [ ] **2. Board of directors — incorporating SOLO for now**
  - You are forming with **one (1) director (yourself)**. Virginia allows this. ✅
  - ⚠️ **Before Track 2 (IRS):** recruit **≥2 more unrelated directors** so the board is ≥3 with a majority unrelated. The IRS disfavors single-person/related boards and can flag/deny a Form 1023 from one. Adding them is a simple board resolution — no Articles amendment needed.
  - You will hold all initial officer roles (President, Secretary, Treasurer) until others join. Same person may sign as different officers.

- [ ] **3. Registered agent — yourself**
  - You qualify as your own agent (VA resident + initial director). ✅
  - ⚠️ Your **home street address becomes public record** on the SCC site. If you'd rather keep it private, switch to a paid registered-agent service (~$50–150/yr) before filing.
  - Record the registered office: your Virginia street address (no P.O. box).

- [ ] **4. Finalize the Articles of Incorporation** → see `01-articles-of-incorporation.md`
  - Fill every `[BRACKET]` placeholder.
  - Confirm the **501(c)(3) purpose clause** and **dissolution clause** are intact (the IRS requires these — the default SCC form omits them).

- [ ] **5. File the Articles with the SCC**
  - Online via Clerk's Information System (CIS): https://cis.scc.virginia.gov
  - Form: **Articles of Incorporation of a Virginia Nonstock Corporation (SCC819)** — or file the custom version with the IRS language attached.
  - Fee: **~$75** (verify current amount). Pay online.
  - Save the stamped/approved Articles + the certificate of incorporation (PDF) — you'll need these for the EIN, bank, and IRS 1023.

- [ ] **6. Adopt the Bylaws** → see `02-bylaws.md`
  - Fill placeholders. These are NOT filed with the state; keep with corporate records.

- [ ] **7. Hold the organizational meeting**
  - Agenda template: `03-organizational-meeting-minutes.md`
  - Adopt bylaws, elect directors/officers, adopt the **Conflict of Interest Policy** (`04-conflict-of-interest-policy.md`), authorize bank account + EIN application.
  - Have the Secretary sign the minutes; keep them.

- [ ] **8. Start the corporate records binder**
  - Articles (approved) · Bylaws · COI policy (signed by each director) · Meeting minutes · Director/officer roster · EIN letter (Track 2).

## What carries into Track 2 (IRS) and Track 3 (state follow-ups)

| Needed for | Item produced in Track 1 |
|---|---|
| EIN (IRS SS-4) | Approved Articles + officer info |
| Form 1023 / 1023-EZ | Articles (with IRS clauses), Bylaws, COI policy, board roster |
| Bank account | Articles, Bylaws, EIN |
| VDACS charitable registration | Articles, board roster, financials |
