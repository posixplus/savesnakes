🏡 Yard Safety

How to make your yard less inviting to snakes

How to Avoid Snakes at Your Yard & Home

Snakes are usually harmless and avoid people — but here's how to make your yard less inviting to them:

  • 🧹
    Keep your yard clean — remove brush, wood piles, and tall grass
  • 🚪
    Seal gaps — close cracks in foundations, doors, and vents
  • 🐭
    Control rodents — less food means less reason for snakes to visit
  • 💧
    Fix leaks — reduce standing water that attracts prey like frogs and insects
  • 🌱
    Trim shrubs and mulch lightly — snakes love hidden, cool spots
  • 🧼
    Keep pet food indoors — it can attract small animals that snakes hunt

💡 Good to Know

None of the commercial snake deterrents actually work! Most snakes in our area are non-venomous and helpful (they eat pests!). It's always best to give them space. Stay safe and snake-smart!
