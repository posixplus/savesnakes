Free Snake Rescue Services in Richmond

Find the approved relocators here: freesnakerelocation.com

Some of these Richmond Virginia Government Agencies may help with wildlife issues. Facing a wildlife problem in Richmond? These agencies might help at no cost. Here's what each one does and how to approach them:

Henrico County Animal Services

(804) 646-5573

Mainly handle domestic animal concerns (stray dogs, dangerous pets etc), but they assist with wildlife issues too.

Richmond Wildlife Rehabilitation Clinic

804-378-2000

A private, donation-funded organization for injured animals.

Virginia Wildlife Commission

804-367-0909

Equipped to deal with larger wildlife like bears or cougars, but they can help with snakes too.

Richmond Police Department

804-646-8092

Richmond police sometimes help with wildlife problems too!
